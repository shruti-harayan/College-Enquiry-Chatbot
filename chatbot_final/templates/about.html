About Ramanand Arya D.A.V College

Ramanand Arya D.A.V College (RADAV) is a premier educational institution established with the aim of providing quality education to students. The college is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

RADAV College has been accredited with 'A' grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and has been recognized as one of the best colleges by the University of Mumbai. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Commerce, and Science disciplines.

Our mission is to provide students with a holistic education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also on character building, skill development, and fostering values that prepare them to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

The college has state-of-the-art infrastructure including well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, sports facilities, and modern ICT-enabled classrooms to provide students with the best possible learning environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education that is recognized nationally and internationally for its academic excellence, innovative research, and commitment to producing graduates who are capable of making significant contributions to society.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education that is accessible, inclusive, and transformative.
  • To foster a culture of inquiry, innovation, and intellectual growth.
  • To promote ethical values, social responsibility, and global citizenship.
  • To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and attitudes necessary for personal and professional success.
  • To contribute to the advancement of knowledge through research and scholarship.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

We are committed to maintaining high standards of teaching, learning, and research.

Inclusivity

We value diversity and create an environment where everyone feels welcomed and respected.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, problem-solving, and the exploration of new ideas.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions and decisions.

Global Perspective

We prepare our students to be responsible global citizens who can thrive in a interconnected world.

Social Responsibility

We are committed to making positive contributions to society and addressing pressing societal challenges.
